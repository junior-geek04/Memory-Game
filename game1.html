Instructions

  • As you click on "Start Game" button a new page will open and the Timer starts immediately.
  • You need to find the match of images in 1 Min.
  • Please fill your details to Play the Game.
  • After 60 sec You can continue your game your total time will be displayed in time taken.
  • To start a new game you can click on Play Again or you can refresh the page.
  • The total number of counts (how many times you turned your card) will also be displayed.
